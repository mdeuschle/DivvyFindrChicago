The Windy City's favorite app to locate Divvy Bikes. What are Divvy Bikes and why are they so cool? Divvy Bikes are bikes in downtown Chicago you can rent for 24hrs. The bike stations are scattered throughout the city. It's pretty awesome. But, locating bikes can be a challenge...until now.

  1. View all Divvy Bike Stations in a list sorted by distance from current location
  2. Number of bikes available to rent at each Divvy Bike Station
  3. Filtered search bar
  4. Map View with current location and selection Divvy Bike Station
  5. Step-by-step directions to Divvy Bike Station from current location
  6. Clean, modern, and efficient design
